Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2lbs cream

Method:

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat
  2. gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. once butter is melted. add cream and eggs
  4. using a spatula, fold the eggs from the edge to the center every 20 seconds. (as if you are making an omlette)
  5. when the eggs are still moist remove from heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
